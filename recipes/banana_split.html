Banana Split

Decription

This is my favorite recipe for a wonderfully simple banana split.

Ingredients

  • 3 (1/4 cup) scoops vanilla ice cream, or to taste
  • 2 almonds, chopped
  • 1 fresh strawberry, sliced
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons whipped cream, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 tablespoons strawberry syrup, or to taste

Steps

  1. Place ice cream scoops in a row in an oblong dish.
  2. Sprinkle almonds around the ice cream, then strawberry slices, and then banana slices.
  3. Place 1 cherry on top of each ice cream ball except the middle one.
  4. Put whipped cream on the middle ice cream scoop and top with remaining cherry.
  5. Drizzle with strawberry syrup.
  6. Serve immediately.
